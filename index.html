Our Story: From a Simple Idea to a Social Movement

CartLu was born from a simple observation: while cities modernized, the local fruit and vegetable hawkers—the very soul of our daily markets—were being left behind. We saw their struggle, their resilience, and their invaluable contribution to our communities.

Our mission is to empower these micro-entrepreneurs with simple, effective technology. We provide them a digital platform to showcase their produce, connect with a wider customer base, and streamline their daily operations. For you, it means convenient access to the freshest produce and the joy of supporting a local business owner directly.

Simple Steps to Freshness

Connecting with your local hawker has never been easier. Experience a seamless journey from cart to kitchen.

1. Hawkers Go Digital

Local vendors easily list their fresh, daily produce and location on the CartLu app, creating their own digital storefront in minutes.

2. You Discover & Order

Browse nearby hawkers, view their real-time inventory, and place your order for the freshest fruits and vegetables through our app.

3. Quick Pickup or Delivery

Choose convenient home delivery or a quick pickup from your favorite vendor's location. Freshness, guaranteed.

4. Community Thrives

With every order, you directly support a local family, reduce food waste, and help preserve the vibrant market culture of India.
